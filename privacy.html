Privacy Policy

For the mobile game Legacy of the Sphinx, developed and published by Afrisoft AS.

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Legacy of the Sphinx.

Information We Collect

Legacy of the Sphinx does not currently collect personal information from users. The game does not require account registration and does not request your name, email address, phone number or precise location.

The game does not access your contacts, photos, camera or microphone.

Gameplay Data

Game progress is stored locally on your device unless your operating system provides its own backup functionality. Afrisoft AS does not receive or store your gameplay progress on its own servers.

Advertising

Legacy of the Sphinx does not currently display advertisements.

In-App Purchases

The game does not currently offer in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

At the time of publication, the game does not use third-party analytics, advertising or tracking services that collect personal information. If such services are introduced in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

Children's Privacy

Legacy of the Sphinx is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. Afrisoft AS does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Because the game does not currently collect personal information, no personal user data is stored on Afrisoft AS servers.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
