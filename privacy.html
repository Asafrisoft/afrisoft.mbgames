Privacy Policy

for Legacy of the Sphinx

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Legacy of the Sphinx is developed and published by Afrisoft AS. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the game.

1. Information We Collect

At this time, Legacy of the Sphinx does not collect personal information from users. The game does not require account registration and does not request your name, email address, phone number or precise location.

The game does not access your contacts, photos, camera or microphone.

2. Gameplay Data

Game progress may be stored locally on your device. Your device operating system may also provide backup functionality. Afrisoft AS does not receive or store gameplay progress on its own servers.

3. Advertising

Legacy of the Sphinx does not currently display advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

Legacy of the Sphinx does not currently offer in-app purchases.

5. Third-Party Services

The current version of the game does not use third-party advertising, analytics or tracking services that collect personal information.

If third-party services are introduced in a future version, this Privacy Policy and the relevant Google Play disclosures will be updated.

6. Children’s Privacy

Legacy of the Sphinx is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. Afrisoft AS does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

Because the game does not collect personal information, Afrisoft AS does not store personal user data on its own servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
